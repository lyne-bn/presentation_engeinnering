Targets visible only in short windows

Attitude / slew limits between shots

No shared real-time global view

Reactivity [4]

Clouds invalidate planned shots

Research questions

Problematic & research questions

Can cooperative, physics-grounded MARL schedule an EO constellation: feasibly, reactively, and within power?
RQ1 Feasibility: Can learned policies respect hard physical constraints, not just soft penalties?
RQ2 Coordination: Can decentralized agents cooperate with no central assignment?
RQ3 Reactivity: Can the fleet respond to clouds and emergent events on the fly?
RQ4 Value: Does it beat classical heuristics: and does the centralized critic (MAPPO) help?
